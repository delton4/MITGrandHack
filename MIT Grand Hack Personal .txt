Track 2: Virtual Diagnostic Interfaces (VDI)
Accurate and timely diagnosis can make the difference between recovery and complication, yet millions still face long waits, limited access, or errors in the process. Studies estimate that diagnostic errors affect about one in twenty adults each year in outpatient settings.² This track challenges innovators to develop connected, easy-to-use diagnostic tools that bring clarity and speed to care. From AI-powered triage systems and virtual imaging platforms to symptom-checking interfaces and telehealth dashboards, participants can create technologies that help providers make faster and more confident decisions. The goal is to make diagnosis more accurate, accessible, and human for everyone.
Source: National Library of Medicine. Diagnostic Errors. https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/24742777/
