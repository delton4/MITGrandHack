The Science

One camera. The body tells us everything.

Thermal image with automated keypoint detection — Zhang et al. (2025), Biomedical Optics Express.
AI identifies chest-abdomen (core) and hand/foot (peripheral) regions to compute CPTD continuously.
CPTD = Tcore − Tperipheral
Sepsis triggers vasoconstriction. Extremities cool while the core stays warm. This gradient is measurable continuously, passively, and without contact by thermal camera.
