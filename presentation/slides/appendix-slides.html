Appendix A2 — HL7v2 Integration (Phase 1)

HL7v2 ORU^R01 Message Format

Edge box emits standard HL7v2 ORU messages on the medical device VLAN. Capsule receives it identically to any bedside monitor. Zero custom development.

// PHASE 1: Raw zone temperatures only (display/store — MDDS) MSH|^~\&|NEOTHERM|NICU_BAY1A|CAPSULE|NORTHWELL|20260315120000||ORU^R01|MSG001|P|2.6 PID|1||MRN12345^^^NORTHWELL||GARCIA^MARIA OBR|1|||NEOTHERM_THERMAL|||20260315120000 OBX|1|NM|CORE_TEMP^Core Temperature||36.8|degC|36.0-37.5||||F OBX|2|NM|L_HAND^Left Hand Temp||33.1|degC|||||F OBX|3|NM|R_HAND^Right Hand Temp||33.6|degC|||||F OBX|4|NM|L_FOOT^Left Foot Temp||31.5|degC|||||F OBX|5|NM|R_FOOT^Right Foot Temp||32.1|degC|||||F // PHASE 2 adds these segments (CPTD computation + trending): OBX|6|NM|CPTD^Core-Peripheral Temp Diff||3.2|degC|<2.0|H|||F OBX|7|CE|ALERT_LEVEL||HIGH||||||F
Transport
TCP/MLLP on hospital medical device VLAN. Same protocol used by every bedside monitor at Northwell.
Routing
Capsule receives → forwards to Health Connect → Health Connect transforms and routes to EHR flowsheet.
Frequency
q15min summaries from continuous stream. Alert-level changes sent immediately. 24h local buffer on edge box.

Appendix A9 — Length-of-Stay Cost Savings Model

20% LOS reduction = $213M/year nationally

Conservative estimate based on LOS reduction alone — excludes mortality reduction, antibiotic savings, and malpractice avoidance.

Source Studies
Swanson et al. (2018) — HeRO Trial
J Pediatrics, 198:162-167. PMID: 29703576
Continuous heart rate characteristics monitoring in VLBW neonates.
Result: 3.2 days LOS reduction for sepsis survivors (P=.026).
83.6% vs 80.1% discharged alive before day 120 (P=.014).
ScienceDirect  |  Analog for NeoTherm: continuous monitoring → earlier intervention → shorter stay
Rolnitsky et al. (2023) — Ontario NICU Costs
Frontiers in Pediatrics, 11:1068367. PMC9941343
12,660 infants, 23–30 weeks GA, all Ontario NICUs, 2010–2017.
Late-onset sepsis: +$32,954 incremental cost, +16 days LOS.
Per-day incremental cost: $32,954 / 16 = ~$2,060/day.
