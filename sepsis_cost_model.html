Baseline Outcomes
LOS mortality rate Mortality among VLBW infants with LOS: 10-18% across studies. Midpoint 15%. % Multi
LOS → shock rate 9% of LOS cases progress to septic shock (requiring vasopressors). Gorantiwar et al. 2021, n=279. % Gor '21
Shock mortality 40% mortality among neonates progressing to septic shock. Gorantiwar et al. 2021. % Gor '21
LOS → meningitis rate ~5% of sepsis-evaluated neonates develop meningitis. Stoll 2004, Flannery 2023. % Stoll '04

Early Detection Effect
Shock prevention rate % of shock cases prevented by earlier treatment. Conservative 30%, moderate 50%, aggressive 70%. Scenario-dependent. %
Mortality reduction HeRO RCT showed 22% mortality reduction in VLBW. We scale by scenario. Moorman et al. 2011, BMJ. % HeRO

Meningitis prevention % of secondary meningitis cases prevented by earlier sepsis treatment. Conservative estimate. %
